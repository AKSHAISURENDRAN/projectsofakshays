Network Design for FIRE DETECTOR

The "Fire Detector" project is a cutting-edge endeavour that aims to revolutionize fire safety and prevention through the development of an intelligent fire alarm system. Traditional fire alarm systems often suffer from false alarms and limited situational awareness, leading to inefficiencies and potential safety risks. In contrast, Smart Fireguard utilizes advanced sensor technologies and real-time data analysis to create a highly responsive and accurate fire detection and prevention system.

TASK MASTER

Task Master is a robust task management application developed using Django, meticulously crafted to simplify personal and team-based task organization. This web-based platform offers a streamlined approach to task creation, assignment, and ultimately boosting productivity and fostering seamless collaboration. With its user-friendly interface, customizable task categories, punctual reminders, and real-time status updates, Task Master empowers both individuals and teams to adeptly oversee their to-do lists, guaranteeing timely task completion and goal attainment.


IPHONE RATING & PRICE ANALYSIS

This study provides an analysis of iPhone prices and ratings, examining the relationship between the cost of various iPhone models and their corresponding consumer and expert ratings. The research utilizes data from reputable technology websites, consumer review platforms, and Apple's official website to gather comprehensive information on iPhone pricing and user satisfaction. The analysis seeks to uncover any correlations between pricing and consumer ratings, shedding light on how price points may influence the perceived value and desirability of different iPhone models.
